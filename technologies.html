    DNA Analysis

    DNA analysis is a key technology used in forensic science to identify suspects, victims, and unknown individuals. There are several methods used for DNA analysis, including:

    PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)

    RFLP (Restriction Fragment Length Polymorphism)

    STR (Short Tandem Repeat) analysis

    Image and Video Analysis

    Forensic scientists use image and video analysis to examine digital images and videos for evidence. This includes enhancing images to make them clearer, recovering deleted or altered images, and analyzing surveillance footage.
